Frequently Asked Questions

Are my images uploaded to any server?

No. PixelPress processes everything inside your browser. Your images stay private and never touch a remote server.

Which image formats can I use?

We support JPEG, PNG, and WebP across most tools. Some tools also work with BMP, TIFF, and SVG. Check each tool's page for specifics.

Is there a file size limit?

We recommend keeping files under 10MB for the best experience. Very large images may slow down processing, particularly on mobile devices.

How does the background remover work?

It uses an AI model that runs directly in your browser. After uploading, the tool automatically detects the subject and removes the background. It works offline once loaded.

Can I use PixelPress on my phone?

Absolutely! All tools are mobile-friendly and work on Android, iPhone, and tablets.

Is PixelPress completely free?

Yes. All tools are free to use. We fund the site through non-intrusive advertising.

Which browsers are supported?

We support Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge, and Samsung Internet. For the best experience, keep your browser updated.

Will you add more tools in the future?

Yes! We're constantly working on new features like batch processing and advanced editing. Stay tuned!
