Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about PixelPress

🔒 Are my images uploaded to any server?
No. PixelPress processes everything inside your browser. Your images, documents, and files stay on your device and are never uploaded to any remote server. You can even disconnect your internet after the page loads and all tools will continue working offline.
🔐 Is my data safe while using PixelPress?
Absolutely. Since all processing is local, no third party can access your files. We use localStorage only for your preferences (theme, language) and recent tool history — this data never leaves your browser and is automatically cleared after 30 days of inactivity.
🖼️ Which image formats are supported?
We fully support JPG, PNG, WebP across all image tools. Some tools also accept AVIF, BMP, TIFF, and SVG. PDF tools work with standard PDF documents. Check each tool's page for specific format support.
📏 Is there a file size limit?
We recommend keeping files under 10MB for the smoothest experience. Larger files may cause slower processing, particularly on mobile devices or older browsers. There's no hard limit — the tool will process whatever your browser can handle.
🫥 How does the background remover work?
Our background remover uses an AI model that runs directly in your browser. When you upload an image, the AI detects the subject and removes the background automatically. It works offline once the model is loaded. Results are best with clear subjects on contrasting backgrounds.
💰 Is PixelPress completely free?
Yes! All 30+ tools are 100% free with no hidden fees, premium walls, or forced sign‑ups. We sustain the website through non‑intrusive advertising. You'll never be asked to pay for any feature.
🌐 Which browsers are supported?
PixelPress works on all modern browsers: Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox, Apple Safari, Microsoft Edge, and Samsung Internet. For the best experience, keep your browser updated to the latest version.
📱 Can I use PixelPress on my phone or tablet?
Absolutely! All tools are fully responsive and work seamlessly on Android phones, iPhones, iPads, and other tablets. The interface adapts to your screen size for easy editing on the go.
🛑 Why is a tool not working on my browser?
Try these steps:
1. Update your browser to the latest version.
2. Disable extensions (ad blockers can sometimes interfere).
3. Clear browser cache and reload the page.
4. Try a different browser (Chrome recommended).
5. Check your internet connection — some tools need to download AI models on first use.
📝 Do I need to create an account?
No. PixelPress requires no registration, no login, and no account. Just visit the website and start using any tool immediately. Your preferences are saved locally in your browser.
🆕 Will you add more tools in the future?
Yes! We're constantly working on new tools and improvements. Planned features include batch processing, advanced AI editing, video tools, and more format support. Stay tuned — all future tools will remain free.
📧 How can I contact support?
You can reach us through our Contact page. We typically respond within 24-48 hours. For urgent issues, you can also reach us on WhatsApp at +92 343 2535207.
