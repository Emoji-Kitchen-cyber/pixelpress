Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Our Privacy Commitment

PixelPress is designed with privacy at its core. All image processing happens directly in your browser. We do not upload, store, or have access to your images. This policy explains what little data we do collect.

2. Information We Don't Collect

  • Your images — they stay on your device and are processed locally.
  • Personal identification information (name, email) unless you voluntarily provide it via our contact form.
  • Files you upload are never transmitted to any server.

3. Automatically Collected Data

Like most websites, we use Google Analytics (via Vercel Analytics or similar) to understand general usage patterns. This collects:

  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Browser type and device category
  • Approximate geographic region (country level)

No personally identifiable information is gathered through analytics.

4. Cookies

We use minimal cookies for functionality (e.g., remembering tool preferences). We also use third-party cookies from Google AdSense to serve relevant ads. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

5. Third-Party Services

We may use:

  • Google AdSense — displays ads based on your interests. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it to serve ads based on your visit to our site and other sites. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
  • Formspree (if used for contact forms) — processes submitted messages on our behalf.

6. External Links

Our site may link to external resources. We are not responsible for their privacy practices.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We will update this page if our practices change. Continued use of the site means you accept the revised policy.

9. Contact

For privacy concerns, contact us at support@pixelpress.app.
