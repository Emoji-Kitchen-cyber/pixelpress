Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

Welcome to PixelPress. Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy document outlines the types of personal information that is received and collected by PixelPress and how it is used.

1. Data Processing (Our Core Commitment)

PixelPress is a browser-based utility platform (Image Compress, PDF Tools, Resizer). All file processing, conversions, and modifications happen directly inside your web browser using client-side scripts. We do not upload, store, or copy your documents or images onto any server. Your files never leave your device.

2. Information We Collect

Since our tools work completely offline/client-side, we do not require you to create an account. However, we may collect minimal non-personal data such as:

  • Log files (browser type, ISP, IP address, page exit/entry paths) to analyze website traffic.
  • Cookies to save your theme preferences or tool configurations.

3. Google AdSense & Analytics

We may use third-party advertising companies (like Google AdSense) to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites.

4. Security

We leverage standard hosting environments (Vercel) to deliver our website scripts securely via HTTPS encryption. Since no file data is ever retained on a database, your document security is completely safe under your own device's control.
