Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

🔒 Key Promise: PixelPress processes all files directly in your browser. Your images, documents, and data never leave your device and are never uploaded to any server.

1. Information We Don't Collect

  • Your files — images, PDFs, documents stay 100% on your device. They are processed locally using your browser's engine.
  • Personal identification — we do not collect your name, email, or phone number unless you voluntarily provide it via our contact form.
  • Uploads — there is no "upload" step. Files are read into browser memory and discarded when you close the tab.

2. Automatically Collected Data

Like most websites, we use Google Analytics to understand general usage patterns. This automatically collects:

  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Browser type and device category
  • Approximate geographic region (country level only)

No personally identifiable information is gathered through analytics. Your IP address is anonymized.

3. Cookies & Local Storage

We use minimal cookies for:

  • Remembering your theme preference (dark/light mode)
  • Remembering your language choice (English/Arabic)

We use localStorage to save your tool history (e.g., shortened URLs, extracted text). This data stays on your device and is automatically cleared after 30 days of inactivity.

4. Third‑Party Services

We use the following trusted services:

  • Google Analytics — anonymous usage statistics.
  • Google AdSense — displays non‑intrusive ads to keep our tools free. Google may use cookies to serve relevant ads. You can opt out at Google Ads Settings.
  • Formspree — processes our contact form submissions. When you send a message, it is forwarded to our email.
  • CDN Providers (Cloudflare, jsDelivr, UNPKG) — deliver JavaScript libraries required for our tools to function.

5. External Links

Our site may contain links to external resources. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of third‑party websites.

6. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of PixelPress after changes means you accept the revised policy.
